Support Channels

What To Include

  • A short description of the problem
  • Which cleanup flow you ran: watch, search, or both
  • Whether the popup showed manual resume or an error code
  • Your Chrome version and operating system
  • Screenshots, if they help explain the issue

Typical Support Topics

  • Account discovery not matching the signed-in Google account
  • YouTube channel selection issues in multi-channel accounts
  • Manual resume after My Activity reauthentication
  • Nothing-to-delete results that look unexpected
  • Store listing or privacy policy questions

Privacy Reminder

Do not include passwords, verification codes, or other sensitive credentials in support requests. If you share screenshots, review them before sending to avoid exposing personal account details you do not want to publish.
